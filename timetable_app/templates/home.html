About TTGS

Academic scheduling,
reimagined.

TTGS (Timetable Generation System) was built to eliminate the time-consuming, error-prone process of manual timetable creation that plagues academic institutions every semester.

Our intelligent scheduling engine considers faculty availability, subject hours, room capacities, and institutional constraints — generating optimized, conflict-free timetables that would take humans days to create manually.

Designed for Indian educational institutions, TTGS supports complex semester structures, multiple departments, and varying faculty workloads — all within a clean, professional interface.

Smart Allocation

Algorithms ensure every subject, faculty, and room is assigned optimally without manual intervention.

Saves Time

What takes a week of manual work is completed in seconds — freeing administrators for higher-value tasks.

Optimized Scheduling

Balanced workload distribution and smart time-slot assignment for an efficient academic experience.

Secure & Reliable

Role-based authentication and data protection ensure only authorized personnel access sensitive schedules.

Process

From setup to schedule
in four steps.

01

Configure Institution

Set up departments, semesters, and academic structure. Input the rules and constraints specific to your institution.

02

Add Faculty & Subjects

Register teaching staff with their qualifications, availability, and subject assignments. Add all courses and lab sessions.

03

Map Rooms & Slots

Define classroom capacities and time slots for each day. Configure lab schedules, breaks, and special periods.

04

Generate & Export

Hit generate. Review the conflict-free timetable, make any manual adjustments, and distribute to faculty and students.
